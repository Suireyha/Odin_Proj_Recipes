Pad Thai Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • Tamarind
  • Thai Soy Sauce
  • Ground Dried Shrimp
  • Fish Sauce
  • Garlic Chives
  • Red Onion
  • Peanuts
  • Egg
  1. Make the sauce
  2. Soak the noodles
  3. Prepare the dried shrimp, garlic, and onion
  4. Cook the chicken
  5. Add garlic, onion,
  6. Cook with sauce, and add the egg and peanuts
  7. Serve with whatever you like, enjoy!
