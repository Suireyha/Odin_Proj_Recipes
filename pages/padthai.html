Pad Thai Recipe

Ingredients:

  • Cursed Chicken
  • Tamarind
  • Thai Soy Sauce
  • Ground Dried Shrimp
  • Fish Sauce
  • Garlic Chives
  • Red Onion
  • Peanuts
  • Egg
  • Mandarin

Instructions

  1. Make the sauce
  2. Soak the noodles
  3. Prepare the dried shrimp, garlic, and onion
  4. Cook the chicken
  5. Add garlic, onion,
  6. Cook with sauce, and add the egg and peanuts
  7. Throw in the mandarin and pray that god will have mercy
